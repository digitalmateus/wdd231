Membership Application

Non Profit

Non-Profit Member – $125/year

Exclusive tier for verified 501(c)(3) organizations.

  • All Bronze Member benefits
  • Access to nonprofit-specific networking events
  • Discounted rates for community outreach booths
  • Opportunities for nonprofit collaboration grants
  • Featured listing in "Community Impact" directory section

Gold Membership

Gold Member – $625/year

Ideal for established businesses ready to lead and influence locally.

  • All Silver Member benefits
  • Rotating homepage spotlight on Chamber website
  • First access to sponsorship opportunities
  • VIP access to select events
  • 25% discount on advertising packages
  • Opportunity to host Chamber mixers or seminars

Silver Membership

Silver Member – $275/year

Great for small to mid-sized businesses seeking growth and exposure.

  • All Bronze Member benefits
  • Enhanced listing with logo and link on Chamber site
  • 10% discount on booth space at Chamber events
  • Member spotlight in newsletter (once per year)
  • Priority referral placement from Chamber inquiries
  • Access to monthly member training/workshops

Bronze Membership

Bronze Member – $95/year

Best for solopreneurs or community supporters who want to stay connected.

  • Access to 4 networking events per year
  • Business listing on the Chamber website
  • Chamber decal for storefront/website
  • Monthly community newsletter
  • Eligibility for Chamber volunteer committees
